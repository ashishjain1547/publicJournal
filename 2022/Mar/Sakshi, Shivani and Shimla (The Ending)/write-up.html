Excursion 1: Kufri

Kufri had horse riding, bungee jumping, trail and all. It was something I thought was worth going for. Rs 7000 - 8000 spent there. The sad part was we had opted for six activities but we did not have the stamina to get our count to even three.

Visit to Mall Road was, in my view, a wastage of time. It is a place where you would find a lot of shops but I was not into shopping.
One thing that does come to mind about the Mall Road was the snow fall. I had neven seen snow fall before. And even though it was like tiny white crystal floating with the wind but it was still snow fall. 

Am having sour taste in my mouth about that trip, cannot really appreciate even that deserves a good word or two.

Sakshi and Navit paid their 5K+ contribution.

On top of that she was expecting me to buy her iPhone 13 Pro Plus, don't remember what other suffixes were there.
