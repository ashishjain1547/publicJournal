Q: Tell about your day.
A:

I woke up in the morning. 
First thing that I did was wished ‘Radhe Radhe’ to my family. 
Then I listened to some Bhajans.
Then I came here for cooking to prepare breakfast and lunch.
After that, I brushed and made tea. Then I had tea and biscuits. 
Then I went to Aiyra’s place for Baby Sitting.
I played there.

Wished: (verb) past tense of Wish
Wish: Kisi cheez ki ichcha karna
Verb: Kriya

Listened: (verb) past tense of Listen
Listen: Sunanaa

Came: (verb) past tense of come
Come: Aanaa

Went: (verb) past tense of go
Go: Jaanaa

Played: (verb) past tense of Play
Play: Khelna

I played there. In the evening, I taught Aiyra and took her to the park. Around 7:30 PM, I came her for cooking dinner.

Once I will reach home, I will play Ludo, I will listen to songs, I will use my phone to view Facebook, I will have dinner, then I will sleep.

Taught: (verb) past tense of Teach
Teach: Padhaanaa

Took: (verb) past tense of Take
