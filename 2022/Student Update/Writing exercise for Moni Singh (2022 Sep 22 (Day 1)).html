My name is Moni Singh. I am from Bihar. I have studied till 10th class. I am married to Guddu Singh. I have two kids. I am a working woman. I am doing a job of baby sitter. I want to study. I want to learn to speak in English. 
During childhood, I loved studies. I was a naughty child. My mom used to scold me but she did not teach me. My mom blames me that I did not study.
My hobbies are singing, having fun with neighborhood children, and I also like to work.

Past participle: studied.
A future tense sentence for the word ‘study’ is: I will study.
