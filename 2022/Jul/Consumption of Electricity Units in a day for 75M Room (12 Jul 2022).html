Assumption: 1 Unit = 1kW per Hour. And: if measure of time is not mentioned then it is 'Hour' by default.


Energy consumption by Fridge: 90W per Hour
Input Specs: 1 Amp, 220V/50Hz

90 W = 90 joule per Hour
90 W = 0.09 kW per Hour

Number of units consumed in a day:
24 * 0.09 = 2.16 kW

Cooler:  15W
Fan:     5W
Light:   24W
Total:   44W per Hour 

44W per Hour = 0.044 kW per Hour

Number of units consumed in a day: 
= 0.044 * 24 = 1.056 kW

Total Units by All Appliances: 2.16 + 1.056 = 3.216 kW


If we assume that if measure of time is not mentioned then it is 'Second' by default.


Energy consumption by Fridge:   90W per second
Input Specs: 1 Amp, 220V/50Hz

90 W = 90 joule per second
90 W = 0.09 kW = 0.09 kJ per second

0.09 kJ per second = 0.09 * 3600 kJ per Hour = 324 kW

Number of units consumed in a day:
24 * 324 = 7776 kW

Cooler:  15W
Fan:     5W
Light:   24W
Total:   44W per second
Number of units consumed in a day: ~3888 kW
